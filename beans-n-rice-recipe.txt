Ingredients:
 - 1 Tbsp olive oil
 - 2 large cloves garlic
 - 1 large red onion, diced
 - 1 stalk celery, diced
 - 1 green bell pepper, diced
 - 2 lbs. red kidney beans
 - 1 tsp. onion powder
 - 1 tsp. salt
 - 1/4 tsp. black pepper
 - 1 tbsp. hot sauce
 - 2 1/2 cups chicken stock
 - 1 cup white rice
 - 1 tbsp. butter
 - 1 tbsp. minced cilantro

Directions:
 1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan, saute garlic, onion, celery, and pepper
    until tender. Stir in kidney beans and hot sauce. Reduce heat and simmer.

 2. Boil chicken stock and stir in rice and butter. Cover and cook on low for 
    20 min. Remove from heat and allow to stand 5 min.

 3. fold rice and beans together and serve.
